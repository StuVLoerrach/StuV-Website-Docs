# Testimonials Section

The Testimonials Section component displays customer reviews, feedback, and success stories.

## Overview

Testimonials sections build trust and credibility by showcasing positive feedback from real customers. They provide social proof and help potential customers understand the value of your products or services.

## Props

| Prop | Type | Description | Default |
|------|------|-------------|---------|
| `variant` | `string` | The variant of the testimonials section to display | `"variant1"` |
| `title` | `string` | The main heading text | `"What Our Customers Say"` |
| `subtitle` | `string` | The secondary heading text | `""` |
| `description` | `string` | The descriptive text | `""` |
| `testimonials` | `Testimonial[]` | Array of testimonials to display | `[]` |
| `backgroundColor` | `string` | Background color of the section | `"bg-white"` |
| `textColor` | `string` | Text color of the section | `"text-gray-900"` |
| `layout` | `"grid" \| "carousel" \| "masonry" \| "featured"` | Layout style | `"grid"` |
| `columns` | `number` | Number of columns in grid layout | `3` |
| `showRatings` | `boolean` | Whether to display star ratings | `true` |
| `showCompanyLogos` | `boolean` | Whether to display company logos | `false` |
| `autoplay` | `boolean` | Whether to autoplay carousel | `true` |

## Usage

\`\`\`tsx

  return (
    <main>
      <TestimonialsSectionBlock
        variant="variant1"
        title="What Our Customers Say"
        subtitle="Real feedback from real customers"
        description="Don't just take our word for it - see what our customers have to say about their experience."
        testimonials={[
          {
            quote: "This product has exceeded all our expectations. The team is responsive and the platform is incredibly reliable.",
            author: "John Doe",
            role: "CEO",
            company: "Example Corp",
            avatar: "/images/testimonials/john.jpg",
            rating: 5
          },
          {
            quote: "We've seen a 200% increase in efficiency since implementing this solution. Highly recommended!",
            author: "Jane Smith",
            role: "Operations Manager",
            company: "Efficiency Inc",
            avatar: "/images/testimonials/jane.jpg",
            rating: 5
          },
          {
            quote: "The best customer service I've ever experienced. They truly care about their customers' success.",
            author: "Bob Johnson",
            role: "Project Manager",
            company: "Success Ltd",
            avatar: "/images/testimonials/bob.jpg",
            rating: 5
          }
        ]}
        layout="grid"
        columns={3}
        showRatings={true}
        backgroundColor="bg-white"
      />
    </main>
  );
}
\`\`\`

## Accessibility

- All testimonials are properly structured with semantic HTML
- Star ratings include text alternatives for screen readers
- Video testimonials include captions and transcripts
- Carousel controls are keyboard accessible
- Focus management follows best practices
- Color contrast meets WCAG AA standards
